This is pong reimagined - with cats! Play against the computer or with a friend. First to 5 points wins. Use the keyboard to control your player.

    For 1-player mode:
  • Up: LEFT or UP arrow keys
  • Down: RIGHT or DOWN arrow keys

    For 2-player mode:
  • Player 1 (left): Up - W or A; Down - S or D
  • Player 2 (right): arrow keys as in player 1 mode
